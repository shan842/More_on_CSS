Google Fonts

Google Fonts is a library of over a thousand free, open-source fonts that you can use on your website. It allows designers and developers to easily embed custom fonts into their web pages, ensuring consistent typography across different browsers and devices.

One of the popular fonts available on Google Fonts is Montserrat. Montserrat is a versatile sans-serif typeface that is widely used for its clean and modern look. It comes in various weights and styles, making it suitable for both headings and body text.

Using Google Fonts is simple. You can browse through the library, select the fonts you like, and add them to your website by including a link in your HTML or importing them into your CSS. This ensures that your chosen fonts are displayed correctly across all platforms.
